## Notes

1. **Shared Voice:** Quinn (Host) and Dr. Viktor Hartmann both use Charon. This works because Quinn is the host and Viktor is a guest - they don't typically speak simultaneously.

2. **Accent Handling:** Gemini TTS supports director's notes for styling. Characters with accents (Viktor's German, JB's Southern) should have that context passed during generation.

3. **Voice Selection Criteria:**
   - Match gender to persona unless artistically intentional
   - Match energy level (calm personas get measured voices, energetic personas get upbeat voices)
   - Consider the voice description's fit with the character's speaking style
